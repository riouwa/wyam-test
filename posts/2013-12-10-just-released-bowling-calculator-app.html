Just Released: Bowling Calculator App

Published on Wednesday, December 11, 2013
Open Source Windows Phone

I just released my second Windows Phone app, Bowling Calculator. It's an open source app that I created when [I gave my talk at TCCC15][3]. If you're a fellow developer, feel free to send me a pull request with any bug fixes or enhancements. I want to keep it focused on the simple use case of scoring a bowling game; I don't need it to track multiple games or keep track of history. It's purpose is to serve as a production-ready sample app and to also be good at what it aims to do.
